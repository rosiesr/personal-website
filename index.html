About Me





I was born and raised in South Florida, and for as long as I can remember, I’ve loved the problem-solving nature and infinite challenges of math and science. Throughout high school, I worked on a math research project and began learning computer science, which led me to major in electrical engineering and computer science at Yale. I’ve always had a passion for community, so I’ve been heavily involved in student council since middle school and currently serve as a Senator and Deputy policy director for the Yale College Council. In my free time, I love playing and watching sports –especially soccer– and love throwing pottery.

Please reach out and feel free to contact me on LinkedIn or through email.
